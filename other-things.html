Other Things

Though I've been primarily focused on product design and solving problems in the urban mobility space, I've made some time for those other projects inbetween.

Icons and Illustrations

Drinks

As Getaround began launching in new cities, each would be kicked off with an opening party. This set of icons was designed for the bar menus at these events. These were designed to follow Getaround's illustrative style.

Tacos & Cacti

This set of illustrations was created for employee appreciation day. moovel celebrated by hosting a breakfast taco bar and each employee received a succulent for their desk. To keep things light-hearted and whimsical, I chose to anthropomorphize the themes.

Logos

RnD

Like many places, moovel's cross-functional teams each have their own unique story. The Research and Development team wanted a logo that was geeky and fun, but also representative of the hardware they build.

YOGA VIBES

This logo was designed for an instructor just starting a business, but would eventually teach yoga on vibration plates. We wanted to keep the logo modern and clean.

Websites

Jobs Page

At Getaround, we realized our job page was lackluster and resulted in a lower number of applicants. This is a redesign of the page to showcase more character and culture at Getaround through company stats, perks, anecdotes, and photos. We also wanted to focus on increasing viewer engagement by adding CTAs and providing relevant content.
