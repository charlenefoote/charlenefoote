I've been carefully moving pixels around for a hot minute—thirteen years to be exact.

For the past eight years I've been designing user experiences—six of those years were focused on urban mobility. Currently, I'm apart of the Remix team building tools that give cities a means to create equitable transportation outcomes. Previously, I worked on a transit ticketing mobile app at moovel and on a carsharing platform at Getaround. Also, I'm a mentor at Designlab.

Just in case you are still reading this, here are some unnecessary facts about me. I was born in a flamingo pink hospital in Honolulu. I'm a gosh darn ex-Floridian. I have 35+ plants living in my home. I saw a UFO and this can be corroborated. I'm a hot dog pundit. Writing with a felt tip pen is my equivolent to scissors gliding through paper. I've been car-free for seven years.
